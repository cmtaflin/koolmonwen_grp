Analysis of the Social Progress Index and Happiness Ratings


The Social Progress Index is measured on a scale 0 to 100, while the Happiness Score is aggregated from its components,
with a total range of 0 to 9.

You will note that the three natural pairs of metrics on the Y and X axes naturally have a correlation.
These natural pairs are:

  1. Social Progress Index (SPI) and Happiness Rating
  2. Free Choice Score and Freedom Rating
  3. Health Score and Life Expectancy Rating
